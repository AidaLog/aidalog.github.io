App Overview

NumNum Plan is your complete meal planning solution that helps you:

  • Track ingredients in your pantry
  • Discover new recipes based on what you have
  • Create and manage shopping lists
  • Find the best deals on ingredients
  • Follow cooking instructions step by step
  • Share your recipes with the community
